Project Background
and Outline

The Madaba Regional Archaeological Museum Project (MRAMP) is an Italian American Jordanian collaboration committed to establishing a new Regional Archaeological Museum in the city of Madaba. The objective of the MRAMP is to archaeologically prepare the area of the Madaba Archaeological Park West, in downtown Madaba, as the location for the new Museum in order to display archaeological materials...
